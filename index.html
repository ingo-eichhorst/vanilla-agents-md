Getting Started

The Vanilla AGENTS.md team takes pride in the fact that it is the most lightweight format available anywhere; using our production-quality deployment strategy, your coding agent will have Vanilla AGENTS.md loaded into context before it even reads your repository.

To use Vanilla AGENTS.md, just place a file with the following name at the root of your repository:

  1. AGENTS.md

When you’re ready to move your project to a production deployment, switch to the much faster method:

  1.  

That’s right — no content at all. Vanilla AGENTS.md is so popular that filesystems have been automatically supporting it for over fifty years.
